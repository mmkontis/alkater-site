# Landing Page Design Prompt — ΑΛΚΑΤΕΡ (Alcater)

Design a modern, professional, single-page landing website for **ΑΛΚΑΤΕΡ (Alcater)**, a Greek construction and public works company headquartered in Igoumenitsa, Thesprotia, in the Epirus region of Greece.

---

## Company Profile

- **Name:** ΑΛΚΑΤΕΡ Α.Ε. (Alkater S.A.)
- **Industry:** Construction, roadworks, public infrastructure
- **Location:** Igoumenitsa (Ηγουμενίτσα), Thesprotia, Epirus, Greece
- **Email:** alkater2024@outlook.com
- **Core services:**
  - Road construction & asphalt paving (Οδοποιία & Ασφαλτοστρώσεις)
  - Road markings & signage (Διαγραμμίσεις)
  - Public works & infrastructure (Δημόσια Έργα & Υποδομές)
  - Building construction (Οικοδομικά Έργα)
  - Earthworks & excavation (Χωματουργικά)
  - Hydraulic works — water & drainage networks (Υδραυλικά Έργα)
  - Concrete & rebar works (Σκυροδέματα & Οπλισμοί)
- **Tagline / slogan:** "Χτίζουμε Υποδομές που Αντέχουν στον Χρόνο" (We Build Infrastructure That Stands the Test of Time)
- **Key stats:** 25+ years of experience, 150+ completed projects, 50+ active partners, 100% deadline compliance

## Design & Interaction Details

- **Animations:** Scroll-triggered fade-up, slide-left, slide-right, scale-in animations. Staggered delays for grid items. Smooth and subtle — not flashy.
- **Hover effects:** Image zoom on project/service cards, button color transitions, underline reveals on nav links.
- **Mobile-first responsive:** All grids collapse to stacked layouts. Hamburger nav. Touch-friendly tap targets.
- **Texture:** Optional subtle noise/grain overlay at very low opacity for a tactile, industrial feel.
- **Photography style:** Real jobsite photos — asphalt being laid, road markings, concrete work, heavy machinery. Slightly desaturated or warm-toned for consistency. Mix of close-ups and wide shots.

---

## Language

- All UI text in **Greek (Ελληνικά)**. The site language is `el`.
- Use formal but approachable tone — professional, not bureaucratic.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router, TypeScript)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Images:** `next/image` for optimized loading
- **Theming:** CSS custom properties for brand colors
- **Animations:** Pure CSS keyframe animations with Intersection Observer triggers (no external animation libraries)
